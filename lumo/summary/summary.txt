Summary: Northfield Community Centre Renovation Project

Timeline: Began either March or April of last year (contradictory across council reports). Completed or nearly completed in December.

Budget: Originally budgeted at £240,000 per some documents, though meeting minutes reference £180,000 as the original figure. No reconciliation between these sources.

Completion Status: Main contractor (Halford & Sons) signed off on the job, however a facilities manager letter dated January indicates several outstanding issues remained.

Attendance Data: Conflicting figures—centre newsletter claims 40% increase, while council data shows only 12% rise.

Political Assessment: Both Councillor Davies ("a great success") and Councillor Webb ("a costly disappointment") served on the same oversight committee despite opposing views.

Funding: Mixed sources (government grant, local business sponsorship, council reserves), but no public document discloses the exact breakdown of contributions.
