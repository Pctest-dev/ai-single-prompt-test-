The Northfield Community Centre renovation project began around March/April last year with initial budgets at £240k or £180k (unclear). Completed in December, but the facilities manager's letter pointed to some outstanding issues in January. The centre has seen a 40% attendance increase according to the newsletter while the council reported a 12% rise. Both Councillor Davies and Councillor Webb praised and criticized the project respectively. Funding came from government grants, local business sponsorship, and council reserves, but the exact breakdown was undisclosed.
