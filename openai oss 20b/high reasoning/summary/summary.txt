The Northfield Community Centre renovation, overseen by Halford & Sons, began in late March or early April of last year and was originally budgeted at £240,000 (some minutes state £180,000). By December the contractor signed off on the work, yet a January letter from the facilities manager notes outstanding issues. Attendance has risen—reported as 40 % in the newsletter but only 12 % according to council data. Councillor Davies praised it as “a great success,” while Councillor Webb called it “a costly disappointment.” Funding came from a government grant, local business sponsorship and council reserves; the precise mix was not disclosed.
