Summary
Start date: Project began in either March or April of last year – reports differ.
Initial budget: £240,000 was cited initially; some minutes note £180,000 as the original figure.
Completion status: Main contractor Halford & Sons signed off in December, yet a January letter from the facilities manager notes several outstanding issues.
Attendance increase: The centre’s newsletter reports a 40 % rise, while council data shows only 12 %.
Council reactions: Councillor Davies called it “a great success”; Councillor Webb described it as “a costly disappointment.”
Funding sources: Mix of government grant, local business sponsorship, and council reserves, but the exact allocation is undisclosed.
