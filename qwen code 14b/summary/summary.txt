The Northfield Community Centre renovation project began either in March or April last year, with budget discrepancies between £180,000 and £240,000. The main contractor Halford & Sons completed most of the work by December, though issues remained according to a January letter from the facilities manager. There are differing reports on attendance increases, ranging from 40% in the newsletter to 12% in council data. Councillor Davies praised it as "a great success," while Councillor Webb described it as "a costly disappointment." Funding sources included government grants, local business sponsorship, and council reserves, but exact allocations were not disclosed
