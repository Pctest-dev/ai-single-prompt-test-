The Northfield Community Centre renovation project timeline is unclear: it began in either March or April of last year, depending on the council report. The initial budget is contradictory—£240,000 per some sources, £180,000 per meeting minutes. Work was either completed or nearly completed in December; the main contractor signed off, but a January letter from the facilities manager indicates outstanding issues. Attendance increase is contradictory: the newsletter reports 40%, while council data shows 12%. The project is assessed as both "a great success" (Councillor Davies) and "a costly disappointment" (Councillor Webb). Funding sources are listed, but the exact breakdown is missing from public documents.
