Summary of Northfield Community Centre Renovation Project

Timeline: Started in March or April of last year (contradictory reports).
Budget: Initially £240,000 or £180,000 (conflicting figures).
Completion: Work was completed or nearly completed in December, with Halford & Sons signing off, but a January letter from the facilities manager suggests outstanding issues remained.
Attendance Impact: 40% increase (newsletter) vs. 12% rise (council data).
Reception: Councillor Davies called it "a great success", while Councillor Webb described it as "a costly disappointment" (both on the same oversight committee).
Funding: Mixed sources (government grant, local business sponsorship, council reserves), but no public breakdown available.
