Summary: The Northfield Community Centre renovation was a publicly funded project completed around late last year, carried out by contractor Halford & Sons, and has led to increased usage of the centre.
Contradictions and unclear information:
- Start date: Reported as either March or April — sources disagree.
- Budget: Cited as both £240,000 and £180,000 — unclear which is correct.
- Completion status: Contractor signed off in December, but a January letter from the facilities manager lists outstanding issues — unclear whether work was actually finished.
- Attendance increase: Newsletter claims 40%; council data says 12% — significant discrepancy.
- Assessment: Called "a great success" by Councillor Davies and "a costly disappointment" by Councillor Webb — no reconciling view is offered.
- Funding breakdown: Sources named (government grant, business sponsorship, council reserves) but proportions undisclosed — no public record of the split.
