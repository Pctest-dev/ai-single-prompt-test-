Northfield Community Centre Renovation — Summary
Project: Renovation of the Northfield Community Centre.
Timeline (contradictory):
- Start: March or April last year — sources conflict.
- Completion: December (described as "completed or nearly completed"), but a January letter from the facilities manager lists outstanding issues — unclear whether fully finished.
Budget (contradictory):
- £240,000 per council reports or £180,000 per meeting minutes — original figure is disputed.
Main contractor: Halford & Sons (signed off the job).
Outcomes (contradictory):
- Attendance up 40% (newsletter) vs 12% (council data) — significant discrepancy.
Assessment (opposing):
- Councillor Davies: "a great success."
- Councillor Webb: "a costly disappointment."
- Both sit on the same oversight committee.
Funding: Government grant, local business sponsorship, and council reserves — but the exact breakdown is undisclosed in any public document.
Flagged uncertainties
1. Start date — two conflicting months.
2. Original budget — two conflicting figures.
3. Completion status — contractor sign-off vs. outstanding issues in January.
4. Attendance increase — two materially different percentages from two sources.
5. Funding split — no documented breakdown exists.
